### About me

<p align="justify">
I received my bachelor's degree<sup>1</sup> in <a href="https://geesufmg.com/"> Systems Engineering</a> from <a href="https://ufmg.br/"> Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais</a> in 2019 and I received my master's degree<sup>2</sup> in Computational Intelligence from <a href="https://ufmg.br/"> Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais</a> in 2021. I'm currently a PhD student in Optimization at <a href="https://ufmg.br/"> Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais</a> and working as Data Scientist at <a href="https://bigdata.com.br/en/">Big Data</a>. My main interests are computer vision, data science, machine learning and optimization. In my free time, I like training Brazilian jiu jitsu, skateboarding, traveling and cooking. Feel free to get in touch!
